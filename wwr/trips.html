Trip Packages

Sunshine Rapids (Beginner)

Perfect for families and first-timers, this half-day float takes you through gentle rapids with breathtaking views. Includes snacks and safety gear.

Raging River Run (Intermediate)

A full-day adventure featuring Class III rapids, swimming holes, and a riverside lunch. Best for thrill-seekers ready for some excitement.

Thunder Gorge Extreme (Advanced)

Push your limits on this 2-day expedition through Class IV-V rapids. Camp overnight by the river under the stars. Gear, meals, and guide included.
